welcome to Marino's Bakeshop

Marino's bakeshop

Nestled near Bayanan Public Market in Muntinlupa City, Marino’s Bakeshop has been a beloved neighborhood bakery, bringing warmth and sweetness to every home. Our passion for baking shines through in every loaf, pastry, and cake we create. We take pride in offering a wide variety of freshly baked goods, from soft, fluffy breads to our signature egg pies and specialty cakes. Whether you’re craving a morning treat or a centerpiece for a celebration, we have something special for you. At Marino’s Bakeshop, we also accept pre-orders for custom cakes! To ensure the highest quality and freshness, please place your orders at least 2 to 3 days before pick-up. Come visit us and experience the taste of tradition, baked fresh every day!
